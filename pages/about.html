Our Story

At T Plus Technologies, founded in 2021, we stand at the forefront of electro-mechanical innovation, transforming complex industrial challenges into smart, reliable, and future-ready solutions. Evolving from a small engineering firm into a leading global provider, we specialize in cutting-edge products that blend advanced electronics with precision mechanical engineering. Our diverse portfolio spans industrial automation, consumer electronics, telecommunications, and customized engineering solutions, each designed to optimize processes, enhance productivity, and drive growth.

Backed by a strong R&D foundation and a passionate team of engineers, we continuously harness emerging technologies to meet evolving market demands.

With a steadfast commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction, we partner with businesses worldwide, empowering them to achieve lasting success through the transformative power of technology.
